About US

Yamaha Motor, established in 1955, initially started as a manufacturer of motorcycles. Over the years, the company expanded its offerings and ventured into the production of other motorized vehicles, such as scooters, off-road bikes, ATVs (all-terrain vehicles), and personal watercraft. Yamaha Motor's commitment to innovation and engineering excellence has propelled the company to become one of the leading players in the global automotive industry.
